Frequently Asked Questions

How many lessons will I need to pass?
According to the DVSA, the average is around 45 hours of professional tuition with around 20 hours of private practice. However, everyone learns at a different pace. Use our Lesson Estimator above for a personalised estimate!
Do you offer mock driving tests?
Yes! We conduct mock tests on actual Heckmondwike test routes to ensure you are 100% prepared for the real thing.
What do I need for my first lesson?
You must bring your provisional driving license. We also recommend wearing comfortable, flat-soled shoes to help you get used to the Yaris' clutch and pedals.
Can I book a 2-hour lesson?
Absolutely. Most students find 2-hour blocks more effective for progress as it allows more time to practice complex junctions and manoeuvres.
