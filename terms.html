  • You are responsible for providing accurate contact details.
  • We may refuse or stop a lesson if it is unsafe or inappropriate to proceed.

  • Payment is required in advance unless agreed otherwise.

5. Punctuality

If you are late, lesson time may be reduced. If we are late, we will extend the lesson where possible or make up the time.
